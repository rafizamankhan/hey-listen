# HeyListen 

Local voice dictation for macOS. Powered by OpenAI Whisper. 
Your audio belongs in your machine. 

2.28.26: v0 Early development phase. Not ready for general use yet - but watch this space. 

## How does HeyListen work? 

Hold a hotkey, speak, release. Your words appear wherever your cursor is. 
No cloud. No account. No subscription, No telemetry. 

Built as a FOSS alternative to tools like Wispr Flow.
